- Euler's Method:
	- To solve $\frac{dy}{dx} = f(x,y), y(x_0) = y_0$, choose a step size $\varepsilon$, and let $x_{n+1} = x_0 + n\varepsilon$. Then $$y_{n+1} = y_n + \varepsilon f(x_n, y_n)$$
