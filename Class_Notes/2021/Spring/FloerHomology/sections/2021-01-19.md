:::{.theorem title="?"}
Suppose $f ^{-1} ([a, b])$ contains exactly one critical point $p$ with $\ind(p) = \lambda$ and $f(p) = c$. 
Then
\[
M_b = M_a \union \qty{ D^ \lambda \cross D^{n - \lambda} }
\]
where $n \da \dim M$.
