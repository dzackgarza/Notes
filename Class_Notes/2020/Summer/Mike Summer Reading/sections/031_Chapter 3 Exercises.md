## Recommended Problems 

Exercise (3-7)
:   Let $p\in M$ and $C_p^\infty(M, \RR)$ be the $\RR\dash$algebra of germs of functions at $p$.
    Let $D_p M$ denote the vector space of derivations of $C_p^\infty(M, \RR)$.
    Show that the map

    \begin{align*}
    \Phi: D_p M &\to T_p M \\
    \qty{\Phi_v} f \definedas v([f]_p)
    \end{align*}
    is an isomorphism.
