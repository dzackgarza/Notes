## Dehn Twists

:::{.claim}
Let $A \da \ts{z\in \CC \st 1\leq \abs z \leq 2}$, then $\MCG(A) \cong \ZZ$, generated by the map
\[  
\tau_0: \CC &\to \CC \\
z & \mapsto \exp{2\pi i \abs z}\, z
.\]

:::
