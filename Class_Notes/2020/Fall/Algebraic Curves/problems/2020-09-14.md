:::{.lemma}
Let $A\leq  B$ in $\Div(K)$.
Then

a. $\mathcal{L}(A) \subset_k \mathcal{L}(B)$.
b. We have $\dim \mathcal{L}(B) / \mathcal{L}(A) \leq \deg(B) - \deg(A) = \deg(B-A)$.
:::
