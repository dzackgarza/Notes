What is $\mcf$?
\begin{align*}
\mcf: \mcp^{1, p}(x, y) &\to \mcl^p(x, y) \\
\mathcal{F}(u) &= \frac{\partial u}{\partial s}+J(u) \frac{\partial u}{\partial t}+\operatorname{grad} H_{t}(u)
.\end{align*}
