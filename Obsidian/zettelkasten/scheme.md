- Definition: An **affine scheme** is a locally ringed space $X\congas{\Ringedspace}\spec R$ for some $R\in \CRing$. 
	- A **scheme** is a locally ringed space $X$ with an open cover $\mathcal{U} \covers X$ with each $U_i$ an affine scheme.
