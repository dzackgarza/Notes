$$
\Gamma: \loc\RingedSpace \to \CRing \quad (X, \OO_X) \mapsto \Gamma(X, \OO_X) := \OO_X(X)
$$

$$
\Gamma: \Op(X) \cross \Sh_{/X}(\kalg) \to \kalg
$$

$$
\Gamma(X, \wait): \Sh_{/X}(\kalg) \to \kalg
